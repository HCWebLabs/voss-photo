About Me

Hello, I'm Elena

I'm a portrait and lifestyle photographer based in the heart of the city, with a passion for capturing authentic moments and the quiet beauty of everyday life.

My approach is simple: create a comfortable space where genuine expressions can unfold naturally. Whether it's a intimate portrait session or documenting your brand's story, I believe the best photographs come from real connection.

When I'm not behind the camera, you'll find me exploring local coffee shops, hunting for the perfect light, or planning my next adventure.
